WORK ABOUT

I have worked for fashion companies designing and developing products for over 8 years. During the course of this time, I discovered my growing passion for communication and digital design, which led me to do a course in UX/UI and to create products with which users can connect with. My experience in design and development has instilled in me the importance of analyzing user behaviour in order to create impactful products. This has honed my ability to thoughtfully balance user and business needs in order to bring beautiful ideas to life. I have lived and worked in multiple cities in India, as well as different countries (India, Canada and currently the U.S). This has sharpened my skills so I incorporate empathy - knowing, understanding, and respecting customers/peers - to effectively collaborate. I love exploring - to experience, learn and grow!
